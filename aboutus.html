About IDK

Here at IDK (Items Decently Kreated), our workers dedicate themselves to our company, making sure we do the very best that we can! We work to sell affordable, but amazing items you can't get anywhere else! We've recently started anew after we lost our old factory building in a tradgety... a fire... that made us lose many workers and our entire old building. Now, we have many brand new, young workers, and a brand new building! Buying from us helps us out a lot to keep our company going, and feed our employes! Of course, you can also help out by working here!
