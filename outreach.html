Community Outreach

At IDK, we do try our best to support other companies and communities! Of course, we often donate to various nature conservancy places, to even out the damage our own factories make to the enviornment (please dont boycott us). We also donate for the well being of mankind.

What do we donate to?

Nature Conservation

  • Clean Air Task Force, As said, we donate to places such as Clean Air Task force for the better good of the enviornment! (Even though we may be one of the things they're trying to prevent). They can work to fix the mistakes we've made, and we can keep doing our own thing!
  • Cool Earth, Because we think Earth is COOL!
  • Water.org, At IDK, water is very important to us so that we can keep our workers up and going! Donating to Water.org helps us, too! (if you want to help us in another way, buy our dehydrated water)
